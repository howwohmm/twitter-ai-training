Most people use ChatGPT wrong.

Here's how to 10x your outputs:

The problem: Generic prompts = generic results

The solution: Context stacking

Here's the framework:

1. Give it a role
"You are a senior marketing strategist with 10 years experience"

2. Define the outcome
"Create a viral Twitter thread that will get 1000+ likes"

3. Provide examples
"Like this: [paste example]"

4. Add constraints
"280 characters max per tweet, 10 tweets total"

5. Iterate
Ask for 5 variations, pick the best

This simple shift 10x'd my AI usage.

Try it.
